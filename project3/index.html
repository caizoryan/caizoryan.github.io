Too Long Didn't Listen ((at all))

Is a video archive of interview excerpts revolving around the topic, "Why do you love conversation?" The work takes the form of a modular narrative and encourages the viewer create their own path through the archive, choosing which interviews to watch and in what order. In hopes that each visitor gets to explore the topic of conversation in their own way, and to discover their own reasons for loving this most fundamental of human experiences.
